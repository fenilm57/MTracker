Privacy Policy for MTracker

1. Introduction

Welcome to MTracker ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MTracker (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

Important: By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

MTracker is designed as a local expense tracking application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data remains on your device.

2.2 Usage Data

We do not collect usage data, analytics, or tracking information from the App.

2.3 Device Information

The App may access device features necessary for functionality, such as:

  • Local storage for saving your expense data
  • Camera (if you choose to add photos to transactions - feature may be added in future updates)
  • File system access for PDF export functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your financial data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your transaction information
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • All processing happens locally on your device

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This includes:

  • Transaction records (income and expenses)
  • Categories and descriptions
  • Dates and amounts
  • Any other data you input into the App

4.2 Data Security

While we implement reasonable security measures within the App, the security of your data also depends on:

  • Your device's security settings
  • Physical access to your device
  • Your device's operating system security

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties because:

  • We do not have access to your data
  • All data remains on your device
  • No data transmission occurs to external servers

6. Third-Party Services

MTracker does not integrate with third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The App operates independently without external connections.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since all data is stored locally and we have no access to user data, we cannot determine the age of our users.

8. Data Backup and Loss

Important Disclaimer: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for backing up your data. We are not liable for any data loss due to device failure, uninstallation, or other circumstances.

To protect your data:

  • Regularly export your data using the PDF export feature
  • Use your device's backup capabilities
  • Keep records of important transactions

9. Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the App
  • Modification: You can edit or delete any transaction
  • Export: You can export your data as PDF
  • Deletion: You can delete individual transactions or uninstall the App to remove all data
